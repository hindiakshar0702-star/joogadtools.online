Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about this tool and our platform.

Is the Png Trimmer free to use?

Yes, the Png Trimmer is completely free. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or limits on how much you can use it.

Do I need to upload my data to use the Png Trimmer?

No. At JoogadTools, all processing happens entirely within your web browser (client-side). Your data and files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What are the key features of the Png Trimmer?

Trim transparent pixels from images in bulk. It is designed to be fast, ad-free, and highly reliable without requiring any sign-ups.

🔐 100% Client-Side Processing

This tool uses your device's browser to analyze pixels and crop your images locally. Your actual files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and speed.
